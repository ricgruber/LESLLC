OUR MISSION

A SHARED VISION


LES Music, LLC - 312 Music Group's mission is to strengthen the Chicago music scene by building stronger networks amongst artists, producers, promoters and venues.

Our goal is to help launch careers that will be known for their longevity while using our team to protect creative rights of artists and producers.

What separates our team from the competition is our commitment to artist and brand development and our ability to execute through our trained and experienced producers, engineers, business, marketing and legal team.
